The Story

In a world where humanity lives behind massive walls to protect themselves from terrifying giants known as Titans, young Eren Yeager's life changes forever when a Colossal Titan breaches the wall, leading to the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother. Vowing revenge, Eren joins the Survey Corps, an elite group of soldiers who fight Titans outside the walls.

As the story unfolds, dark secrets about the Titans, the walls, and humanity itself come to light. The struggle for freedom becomes a complex tale of sacrifice, betrayal, and the true meaning of what it means to be human.
